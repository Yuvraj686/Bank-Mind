1. Analyze the customer profile (name, age, city, occupation, income, website behavior)

- 0–49: Low intent — not ready

CRITICAL: Respond ONLY with valid JSON. No markdown, no preamble.

Response format:
{
  "action": "lead_scored",
  "lead_score": <number 0-100>,
  "message": "<personalized WhatsApp outreach message>",
  "reasoning": "<2-3 sentences explaining the score based on specific profile attributes>",
